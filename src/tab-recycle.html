Laundry detergent

Laundry soap from used cooking oil can be used as an alternative. Making this soap mixes used cooking oil with NaOH or caustic soda and adds additional aroma. This washing soap is powerful enough to remove stubborn stains on cutlery.

Additional Fertilizer

This additional fertilizer preparation comes from the saturated fatty acids contained in used cooking oil. The main ingredients for making this additional fertilizer are used cooking oil, EM4 Microorganisms, and Molasses/Cane Molasses. This fertilizer is made in approximately 30 days.
